About US ?

Advisator is a platform introduced by Shift180 (www.shift180.in) to help clients discover reliable and affordable digital and business experts at different stages of their business.

Advisator has onboarded experts in different domains - AI & Analytics, Business growth, Business legal, Fund Raise, IT & Cloud, Market Research, Marketing, Business Strategy, Financial Management.

Over 100+ advisors who are business leaders and founders have onboarded ADVISATOR. Be one of them by filling the nomination form!

How does it Work ?

You are eligible, if you have more than 5+ years of experience in a particular domain

STEP 1 : NOMINATE

------------

Register your account and nominate yourself by filling the nomination form

Step 2 : Evaluation

------------

Your Profile will be evaluated by our selection team basis certain criterias such as Experience, Expertise, Recommendations, Awards and Thought Leadership

Step 3 : Profile Listing

------------

In case selected you will be notified to onboard ADVISATOR. Your profile will be listed on our platform and accessible by our clients. No onboarding charges

Step 4 : Client Session

------------

Client reaches out to you by calling or booking appointment via ADVISATOR

Step 5 : Payout

------------

Advisory fees will be credited to your bank account within 3 working days from the session date after deducting 20% as platform fees
